Introduction.

What's the meaning of talent

The word talent refers to the special skills or abilities that got has given different people Different people have different talents. God created us in different;colours(brown,white,black, etc) and also in different tribes ( Kikuyu, Kisii,Kalenjin, etc). Some talents can be seen by what people do with their bodies physically while other talents are seen in a person's mental capacity.

Examples of talents.

  • Public Speaking.
  • Writing.
  • Singing.
  • Networking (person to person)
  • Perfoming magic.
  • Modelling.

Uses of talents

  1. Source of income.

    2. ie: One can make money out of writing poems,songs,articles.

  2. People can benefit from them.

    3. ie: Some songs give people hope, some encourage and some give advice.

  3. Makes one leave a good impression to people.

    4. ie: People get impressed and excited when you perfom magic.

    Conclusion

    Every human being has her/his inner ability that the Lord has put in them. It's our duty to try find our talents and make use of them. Like the Bible says, in the judgement day, God will ask us how we used our talents. WE should use our special abilities with respect to people and to Glorify God's name.
