What's the meaning of talent

The word talent refers to the special skills or abilities that got has given different people Different people have different talents. God created us in different;colours(brown,white,black, etc) and also in different tribes ( Kikuyu, Kisii,Kalenjin, etc). Some talents can be seen by what people do with their bodies physically while other talents are seen in a person's mental capacity.

  • Public Speaking.
  • Writing.
  • Self Management.
  • Networking (person to person)
  • Networking (in the virtual world)
  • Critical Thinking.
  • Decision Making.
